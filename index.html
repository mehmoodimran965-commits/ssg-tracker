Diet & Nutrition
🥚 Your Meal Plan (Zero Budget)
Your Goal: Build muscle, increase stamina and endurance for SSG preparation. Focus on high protein, complex carbs and healthy fats from affordable Pakistani foods.
Sehri / Breakfast
3–4 boiled eggs + 2 rotis with desi ghee + 1 glass of doodh (milk). Eggs are your best free protein. Ghee gives sustained energy for morning training.
~30g Protein~60g Carbs~18g Fat
Mid-Morning (after school)
Chana (chickpeas) chaat or leftover daal with roti. Chana is one of the best muscle-building foods available cheaply in Pakistan — high protein, high fibre, slow digesting.
~18g Protein~55g Carbs~8g Fat
Iftar / Lunch
Dates (3–4) + water first. Then daal (masoor or mash) with 2–3 rotis. Daal is your daily protein base — eat it every single day. Add a raw onion or tomato on the side.
~22g Protein~80g Carbs~6g Fat
Dinner
Yakhni (bone broth) soup + sabzi (any vegetable) + 2 rotis. Yakhni is extremely cheap and extremely powerful — it contains collagen, gelatin, and minerals for joint recovery and tendon strength. Drink it every night if possible.
~25g Protein~45g Carbs~10g Fat
💡 Key Rules for SSG Preparation
Water: Drink at least 3 litres per day. Most people training hard in Pakistan are chronically dehydrated. This single change will improve your endurance noticeably within 2 weeks.
Eggs are non-negotiable: Try to eat 3–5 eggs every single day. They are the cheapest complete protein source available to you. Boil them, fry them, make omelette — but eat them daily.
Eat before training: Have at least a roti with egg or chana 30–45 mins before your workout. Training on an empty stomach kills performance and muscle growth.
Yakhni (Bone Broth): Ask your family to make this regularly. Simmer beef or chicken bones for 4–6 hours. Drink one cup daily — it directly strengthens your tendons, ligaments and joints which is critical for pull-up and barbell training.
Avoid: Cold drinks, chips, biscuits, excessive chai, and skipping meals. These directly hurt your training results.
🏋️ Your Workout Plan (Iron Hide)
4x Weekly Workout (Mon / Tue / Thu / Sat)
1. Assisted Pull-ups — 5 sets to failure (use resistance band looped on bar for assist)

2. Bent-over Barbell Rows — 4 sets × 12 reps @ 12kg

3. Zercher Squats — 4 sets × 15 reps (barbell in elbow crooks @ 12kg)

4. Push-ups — 4 sets to max

5. Barbell Curls — 3 sets × 12 reps @ 12kg

6. Skipping Rope — 3 × 3 minute rounds (rest 1 min between)

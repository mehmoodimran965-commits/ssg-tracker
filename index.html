Diet Guide
🥚 Your Meal Plan (Zero Budget)
Your Goal: Build muscle and stamina for SSG. Focus on high protein from affordable Pakistani foods.
Sehri / Breakfast
3–4 boiled eggs + 2 rotis with desi ghee + 1 glass milk. Eggs are your best cheap protein source.
~30g Protein~60g Carbs~18g Fat
Mid-Morning
Chana chaat or daal with roti. Chana is high protein, high fibre, slow digesting — perfect for training.
~18g Protein~55g Carbs~8g Fat
Iftar / Lunch
Dates + water first. Then daal masoor/mash with 2–3 rotis. Eat daal every single day.
~22g Protein~80g Carbs~6g Fat
Dinner
Yakhni (bone broth) soup + sabzi + 2 rotis. Yakhni strengthens tendons and joints — drink nightly.
~25g Protein~45g Carbs~10g Fat
💡 Key Rules
Water: 3+ litres daily. Most training performance issues in Pakistan are dehydration.
Eggs: 3–5 daily, non-negotiable. Cheapest complete protein available to you.
Yakhni: Simmer bones 4–6 hours. One cup daily strengthens tendons and ligaments directly.
Avoid: Cold drinks, chips, biscuits, excessive chai, and skipping meals.
